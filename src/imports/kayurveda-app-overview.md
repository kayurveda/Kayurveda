Kayurveda – an AI-driven Ayurvedic wellness PWA app Millennial wellness enthusiasts who are already buying organic products but want a personalised routine, not generic tips.
The AI Solution The mechanism isn't just "chatbot gives tips". Here's what actually happens: Kayurveda runs a multi-layer intake engine:
1. Dosha Profiler — AI asks 8–10 quick questions (skin type, digestion, sleep, stress, climate) and classifies the user as Vata/Pitta/Kapha or a combination.
2. Symptom → Root Cause Mapping — AI maps symptoms (acne, dandruff, yellow teeth) to Ayurvedic root causes (excess heat/Pitta, gut imbalance, etc.) using a trained knowledge base of classical Ayurvedic texts (Charaka Samhita logic).
3. Personalised Remedy Generation — AI generates ingredient-specific home remedies using items they likely already have (turmeric, neem, rose water, coconut oil) rather than generic advice.
4. Routine Builder — AI stitches remedies into a morning/night routine across face, hair, oral, and body — so nothing conflicts.
5. Progress Check-ins — Weekly AI follow-ups that adjust recommendations based on results The key differentiator: cross-body personalisation. Most apps treat acne separately from hair fall. Kayurveda understands they're often the same root cause (Pitta imbalance) and treats them together.
