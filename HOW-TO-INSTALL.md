# 📱 How to Install Kayurveda on Your Phone

## Why Install?

Installing Kayurveda as an app gives you:
- ✨ **Full-screen experience** - No browser bars
- 🚀 **Faster loading** - Works offline
- 🔔 **Reminders** - Never miss your wellness routine
- 📲 **Home screen icon** - Quick access like any other app
- 💾 **Offline access** - Works without internet

---

## 📱 For iPhone & iPad (iOS)

### Step 1: Open in Safari
- Kayurveda **must** be opened in **Safari** (not Chrome)
- If you're in another browser, copy the link and paste it in Safari

### Step 2: Tap the Share Button
- Look at the bottom of Safari
- Tap the **Share button** (square with an arrow pointing up: □↑)

### Step 3: Add to Home Screen
- Scroll down in the share menu
- Tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
- You'll see a preview with the KAYA logo

### Step 4: Customize & Add
- You can change the name if you want (optional)
- Tap **"Add"** in the top right corner
- Done! The app icon will appear on your home screen

### Step 5: Launch the App
- Find the KAYA icon on your home screen
- Tap it to launch in full-screen mode
- It will look and feel like a native app!

## 🤖 For Android Phones (Chrome)

### Method 1: Automatic Prompt (Easiest)

1. **Open Kayurveda in Chrome**
2. **Wait 3 seconds** - A banner will appear at the bottom
3. **Tap "Install App"**
4. **Tap "Install"** in the popup
5. Done! The app will appear in your app drawer and home screen

### Method 2: Manual Installation

If the prompt doesn't appear, you can install manually:

1. **Open Chrome menu** - Tap the three dots (⋮) in the top right
2. **Tap "Add to Home screen"** or **"Install app"**
3. **Tap "Install"** in the popup
4. Done! The app icon will appear

### 🎥 Visual Guide for Android:
```
Chrome Browser
    ↓
Install Banner Appears
    ↓
Tap "Install App"
    ↓
Tap "Install"
    ↓
✅ App Installed!
```

### Finding Your Installed App:
- Check your **home screen**
- Or open your **app drawer** (all apps)
- Look for the KAYA logo icon

## 💻 For Desktop (Windows, Mac, Linux)

### Chrome / Edge:
1. Open Kayurveda in Chrome or Edge
2. Look for the **install icon** (⊕) in the address bar
3. Click it and select **"Install"**
4. The app will open in its own window

### Alternative:
1. Click the **three dots** (⋮) in the browser menu
2. Select **"Install Kayurveda"** or **"Add to desktop"**
3. Confirm the installation

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

### "I don't see the install prompt" (Android)
**Try these:**
1. Make sure you're using **Chrome browser**
2. Wait at least 3 seconds on the page
3. Try the manual method (Menu → Add to Home screen)
4. Refresh the page and wait again

### "Add to Home Screen option is missing" (iOS)
**Try these:**
1. Make sure you're using **Safari** (not Chrome)
2. Update iOS to the latest version
3. Check if you have enough home screen space
4. Try restarting Safari

### "The app doesn't work offline"
**Try these:**
1. Open the app at least once while online
2. Navigate through a few screens
3. Close the app completely
4. Turn off WiFi/Data and try again

### "I installed it but can't find the icon"
**Check:**
- iPhone: All pages of your home screen
- Android: App drawer (swipe up from home screen)
- Desktop: Start menu (Win) or Launchpad (Mac)

## 🗑️ How to Uninstall

### iPhone/iPad:
1. **Long press** the KAYA icon
2. Tap **"Remove App"**
3. Tap **"Delete App"**

### Android:
1. **Long press** the KAYA icon
2. Drag to **"Uninstall"** or tap **"App info"** → **"Uninstall"**

Or:
1. Go to **Settings** → **Apps**
2. Find **Kayurveda**
3. Tap **"Uninstall"**

### Desktop:
**Chrome/Edge:**
1. Open the app
2. Click the **three dots** (⋮) in the title bar
3. Select **"Uninstall Kayurveda"**
